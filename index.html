About Us

Welcome to the Kembong Cultural Development Association. We are dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Kembong and fostering community development. Our mission is to bring together the people of Kembong, both near and far, to celebrate our traditions, share our stories, and work together to create a brighter future.

The association was founded with a vision of unity, cultural preservation, and community progress. Our dedicated team of members and leaders work tirelessly to organize events, workshops, and activities that showcase Kembong's unique culture and traditions.
